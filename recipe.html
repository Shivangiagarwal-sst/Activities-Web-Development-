Delicious Pizza

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Olives
  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes
  • Jalapeños
  • Broccoli or Cauliflower Florets
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare Dough: Stretch or roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface.
  2. Add Sauce & Cheese: Spread a thin layer of sauce, then top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  3. Add Toppings: Arrange your favorite vegetable toppings evenly over the cheese.
  4. Bake: Place the pizza in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) and bake for 10-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  5. Serve: Let it cool for a minute, then slice and enjoy.
